# Roadie Badge System
> Celebrating every real mile of learning. Effort, persistence, reflection — never just the right answer.

All badges are **RoadChain-verified** with cryptographic proof of how and when they were earned.

---

## Discovery Tier (Weeks 1–4)

### First Aha!
- **Earn**: Reach a genuine discovery moment — explain a concept in your own words or apply a new idea unprompted
- **Minimum**: 1 verified moment in a single session
- **Verification**: Roadie's Socratic engine flags it + student self-confirmation
- **Reward**: 10 RoadCoin + spark animation + first highway landmark
- **Roadie says**: "You just connected the dots yourself — that's the best kind of discovery!"

### Roadie's New Friend
- **Earn**: Complete 5 full guided sessions (8+ min each)
- **Minimum**: 5 sessions, no skipping steps
- **Verification**: Session completion with RoadChain timestamp
- **Reward**: 25 RoadCoin + convoy welcome animation + basic voice customization unlocked
- **Roadie says**: "We've taken five rides together. I'm starting to know how you learn best."

### Checkpoint Pioneer
- **Earn**: Fully complete one learning unit with 80%+ concept mastery
- **Minimum**: All core concepts covered + one "Show Your Work" artifact
- **Verification**: Mastery heatmap + RoadChain-stamped portfolio item
- **Reward**: 50 RoadCoin + checkpoint sign animation + unit on Learning Highway

---

## Mastery Tier (Weeks 2–12+)

### Socratic Scout
- **Earn**: 50 full Socratic sessions where you never request a direct answer
- **Minimum**: 50 sessions, any subjects
- **Verification**: Session analytics + RoadChain interaction log
- **Reward**: 150 RoadCoin + "Deep Thinker" title + explorer compass badge
- **Roadie says**: "You've learned to ask better questions than most adults. That's powerful."

### Concept Conqueror
- **Earn**: 90%+ mastery on an entire subject (heatmap + practice accuracy + portfolio)
- **Minimum**: One complete subject (Fractions, Photosynthesis, World War II, etc.)
- **Verification**: Automated mastery calc + optional teacher review + RoadChain portfolio
- **Reward**: 200 RoadCoin + summit badge with subject icon + shareable Mastery Certificate

### Streak Rider
- **Earn**: 30-day consecutive daily learning streak
- **Minimum**: 30 days, 5+ min per session
- **Verification**: Daily login + session log with RoadChain timestamps
- **Reward**: 300 RoadCoin + speed-line highway animation + 7-day "Turbo Learning" boost

---

## Convoy Tier (Social Learning)

### Team Traveler
- **Earn**: 10 group learning sessions in RoadTrip with other students or agents
- **Minimum**: 10 sessions, 15+ min each, active contribution
- **Verification**: RoadTrip session logs + Shared Memory contribution tracking
- **Reward**: 100 RoadCoin (split with group) + convoy illustration badge

### Mentor Rider
- **Earn**: Help 3 different students reach a verified "aha!" moment
- **Minimum**: 3 verified mentoring moments in shared sessions
- **Verification**: Roadie observation + student confirmation prompt
- **Reward**: 150 RoadCoin (giftable) + "Guide" title + badge: one car helping another up a hill

### Family Highway
- **Earn**: 20 joint sessions with a parent, sibling, or family member
- **Minimum**: 20 shared sessions via linked family profiles
- **Verification**: Family profile linkage + joint session logs
- **Reward**: Family convoy badge + unlocks shared family RoadCoin pool + family highway themes

---

## Legacy Tier (Long-Term Achievement)

### Portfolio Pioneer
- **Earn**: Build a portfolio with 30+ high-quality "Show Your Work" artifacts
- **Minimum**: 30 artifacts with meaningful reflections, multiple subjects
- **Verification**: RoadChain stamps on each artifact + portfolio completeness score
- **Reward**: Elegant logbook badge + exportable "Master Learner Passport" + teacher dashboard priority

### Highway Scholar
- **Earn**: Full mastery (90%+) across 3+ different subjects
- **Minimum**: 3 Concept Conqueror badges
- **Verification**: Cross-subject mastery heatmap + RoadChain portfolio review
- **Reward**: Grand multi-lane highway badge + lifetime 10% RoadCoin multiplier + exclusive "Scholar" agent skin

### Lifelong Rider
- **Earn**: 365 days of consistent learning with 200+ total sessions
- **Minimum**: 200 sessions, no gap longer than 60 days
- **Verification**: Annual session count + RoadChain timestamp audit
- **Reward**: "Endless Highway" sunrise badge + permanent "Legendary Learner" title + custom commemorative certificate

---

## System Rules

- All badges are **non-transferable** but fully shareable with RoadChain proof
- Contribute to overall **Personal Road Map** progression in BlackRoad OS
- Display publicly on BackRoad or keep private — student's choice
- Teachers/parents see anonymized summaries unless student shares specific badges
- All badge earnings feed into **RoadCoin** and can unlock marketplace items
