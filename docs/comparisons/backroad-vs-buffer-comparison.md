## The Bottom Line

Buffer is still one of the simplest, most approachable social schedulers — clean interface, solid AI for captions, reliable queuing, no feature bloat. Perfect for plan-and-post without complexity.

BackRoad does everything Buffer does and more: pulls content from your full creation stack, lets your AI convoy engage and network on autopilot, stamps every post with RoadChain provenance, and pays you RoadCoin for the ride.

Buffer helps you schedule.
BackRoad makes your content and your agents drive themselves — while the whole highway sees, verifies, and rewards what you built.
