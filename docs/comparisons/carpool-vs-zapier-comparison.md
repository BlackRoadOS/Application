## The Bottom Line

Zapier remains the undisputed king of no-code app automation — if you need to move data between Slack, Gmail, Notion, Salesforce, it's still the fastest, most reliable plumber.

CarPool is a different game: it imports your actual AIs, gives them shared long-term memory, lets them talk like a real team, and plugs them into the full BlackRoad desktop with verifiable provenance and rewards.

- **Zapier** = the highway maintenance crew connecting exits between different roads.
- **CarPool** = the hub that loads your entire AI fleet into the same car, gives them a shared map, and lets them drive the whole highway together.

Zapier automates tasks.
CarPool makes your AIs ride together — and the entire road moves faster.
