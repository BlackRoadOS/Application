## The Bottom Line

Intercom is excellent: tools for companies to onboard and support their users at scale. The enterprise helpdesk powering thousands of businesses.

RoadSide is the opposite — and the future of onboarding. Your personal AI co-pilot that sits in the passenger seat on day one, asks a few friendly questions, and silently builds your entire BlackRoad world behind the scenes. CarKeys ready. AIs imported. RoadTrip passengers waiting. OneWay trunk packed. No tours to click through. No setup guides to read.

Intercom helps companies retain their customers.
RoadSide makes sure you never feel like a new customer again.
