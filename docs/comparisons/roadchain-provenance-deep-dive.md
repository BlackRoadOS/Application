# RoadChain Provenance
*Every mile, stamped and sealed.*

---

**Provenance** in RoadChain is your unbreakable digital birth certificate + full life story for anything you create or do on The BlackRoad.

It's the cryptographic proof that shows exactly where something came from, who touched it, when, and that it has never been altered — all anchored permanently to the blockchain. A tamper-proof "chain of custody" that follows every file, message, code commit, AI conversation, export, or action like an invisible GPS stamp on every mile.

---

## How RoadChain Provenance Actually Works

### 1. Instant Automatic Hashing
The moment you create or touch anything — a BlackBoard campaign, a RoadCode commit, a RoadTrip conversation, a Roadie tutoring session, a CarPool hand-off, even a RoadView search result — RoadChain instantly creates a unique cryptographic fingerprint (hash). No manual step. Happens in the background while you drive.

### 2. Immutable Ledger Entry
That hash is written to an immutable ledger (public or private side-chain, your choice). Once on the chain, it can never be changed, deleted, or back-dated. Ever.

### 3. Full Chain-of-Custody Timeline
RoadChain records every subsequent step:
- **Who** (or which AI agent) touched it
- **When** (precise timestamp)
- **What** they did (edited, shared, exported, licensed, etc.)
- **Why** (optional context you or an agent adds)

All visible in a clean, expandable "Proof Chain" sidebar or one-click report.

### 4. One-Click Proof of Existence Badges
Any asset instantly gets a shareable verification badge.

Example:
- You finish a BlackBoard ad campaign → it gets a RoadChain badge.
- You share the link with a client → they click the badge and instantly see: "Created by you on March 29, 2026 at 7:13 PM CDT, never altered, licensed under X terms."
- Clients, teachers, courts, or collaborators verify authenticity in seconds without trusting you or any central party.

### 5. Zero-Knowledge Privacy Layers
You control visibility:
- Keep sensitive details hidden while still proving the asset exists and is unchanged.
- Share only what's needed (e.g., "this homework portfolio was completed by the student" without revealing the actual answers).

### 6. Carbon-Negative Anchoring + Gasless
Every provenance stamp is anchored to major public blockchains (Ethereum, Solana) in a carbon-negative way. RoadCoin covers all transaction costs — you never pay gas.
