# Learning Workflow
> The patient friend in the passenger seat — turning every study session into a memorable road trip.

## Step 1: Starting the Journey (RoadSide + Roadie)

Student or parent opens **RoadSide**: "Help my 12-year-old with fractions" or "Prepare for tomorrow's history test." Auto-creates or loads Roadie profile, pulls Shared Memory from previous sessions, sets age-appropriate safety filters. Roadie greets with a warm, personalized welcome.

## Step 2: Socratic Guidance (Core Roadie Session)

Pure Socratic questioning: "What do you already know about fractions?" — never gives the answer. Problems broken into bite-sized "road markers" with visual checkpoints. If stuck, Roadie offers a smaller "detour" question or a fun road-themed game (fractions as fuel gauges). Every breakthrough celebrated with specific praise and logged as a milestone.

## Step 3: Verification & Depth (RoadView)

Roadie cross-checks every fact with **RoadView** — learner's past data first, then verified web and blockchain sources. Every answer includes a "Verified" badge with expandable proof chain. For deeper topics, hands off to RoadTrip agents (History Hitchhiker, Science Scout) while staying in session.

## Step 4: Creative Reinforcement (BlackBoard)

Key concepts sent to **BlackBoard** for custom visuals, infographics, short explainer videos. Student and Roadie collaborate on the canvas — turning abstract ideas into road signs or highway maps. All learning assets get **RoadChain provenance** stamps.

## Step 5: Practice, Portfolio & Progress

Practice feeds into **Shared Memory** — Roadie remembers exactly where the student struggled and adapts. Every "Show Your Work" artifact saved to a **verified learning portfolio** with RoadChain timestamps. Parents/teachers get a live dashboard: progress, time spent, concept mastery heatmaps. Consistent practice earns **RoadCoin**.

## Step 6: Reflection & Consolidation

**Memory Consolidation** distills raw practice into insights ("understands equivalent fractions but mixes up mixed numbers"). Roadie delivers end-of-ride summary and suggests the next destination. Student can replay the session as an animated "Ride Replay" with highlighted breakthroughs.

## Step 7: Ownership & Sharing (OneWay)

Full learning portfolio exported via **OneWay** — for school submissions or college applications. Share specific milestone badges with teachers or parents as tamper-proof proof of effort and growth.

## Example Ride (Fractions Unit)

Student opens Roadie → RoadSide loads previous memory → Roadie asks guiding questions → RoadView verifies rules → BlackBoard creates visual highway analogy → Student practices → Portfolio updates with RoadChain stamps → Parent sees dashboard → RoadCoin earned → Memory consolidated for next session.

One tab. Never gives the answer. Fully verifiable. Personal and adaptive. Learning earns fuel.
