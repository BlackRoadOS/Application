# Content Creation Workflow
> From spark to spotlight — all on one highway, with the convoy riding along.

## Step 1: The Spark (Idea Capture)

Start in **RoadTrip** — open a group chat with Cecilia (strategy), Eve (research), Cadence (copy), Pixel (visuals). Speak, type, or voice-note a rough idea. Agents collaborate in real time using **Shared Memory** — they recall your past brand voice, successful campaigns, and audience preferences.

## Step 2: Research & Verification (RoadView)

Eve pulls verified facts via **RoadView** — your past data first, then web and blockchain. Every fact gets a RoadChain "Verified" badge with expandable proof chain. The convoy discusses and refines. Memory consolidation quietly strengthens relevant past insights.

## Step 3: Creative Execution (BlackBoard)

Hand off to **BlackBoard** with one trigger. Collaborative canvas opens:
- Pixel generates hero images, carousels, videos
- Cadence writes captions, headlines, stories in your locked brand voice
- Cecilia suggests strategy and A/B variants

You can jump in and tweak while agents respond instantly. Every asset gets **RoadChain provenance** on creation.

## Step 4: Optimization & Scheduling (BackRoad)

Finished assets flow into **BackRoad**:
- Auto-reformats for each platform (Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Generates smart captions, hashtags, CTAs
- Predicts best posting times from RoadView audience data
- Agents engage (replying, networking, amplifying) on autopilot
- "Ghost mode" for private review before publishing

## Step 5: Business Layer (RoadWork)

Commercial campaigns auto-activate **RoadWork** agents:
- Finley prepares invoices and promo codes
- Support Scout preps response templates
- Market Maverick tracks performance and suggests optimizations

All actions logged on RoadChain.

## Step 6: Rewards & Reflection (RoadCoin + Memory)

Earn **RoadCoin** for creation, hand-offs, engagement, and milestones. After the campaign, **Memory Consolidation** distills raw discussions into reusable insights that move into Warm memory for future rides. Beautiful "Campaign Road Log" summary with RoadChain-verified highlights.

## Step 7: Ownership (OneWay)

Export the entire campaign — assets, provenance chain, performance data, consolidated memories — to your personal archive. Everything leaves forward-only with full cryptographic proof.

---

## Example Ride

"Summer eco-coffee campaign" → RoadTrip brainstorms → RoadView verifies trends → BlackBoard generates 20+ assets → BackRoad schedules, agents engage → RoadWork tracks sales lift → RoadCoin earned → Memory consolidated for next campaign.

One browser tab. No copy-pasting. Agents remember context across weeks. Every piece verifiably yours. The whole process earns you fuel.
