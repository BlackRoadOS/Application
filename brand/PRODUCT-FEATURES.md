## Roadie
*The patient friend in the passenger seat.*

Safe, education-first AI tutor that never gives away answers — it guides, questions, and celebrates every "aha!" moment. Trusted by teachers, approved by parents, and loved by kids because it actually helps them learn instead of doing the work for them. Roadie rides shotgun on every homework session, every study night, every curious question.

**Core Features**
- **Pure Socratic Tutoring Engine** — the heart of Roadie. Instead of handing over answers, Roadie uses a full suite of proven Socratic techniques to spark critical thinking and genuine understanding. Every interaction follows a deliberate "road-map" of questions that lead the student to discover the solution themselves.

  **Socratic Techniques Roadie Masters**
  - **Clarifying Questions**: Starts every session with gentle probes like "What do you already know about this?" or "Can you walk me through what the question is really asking?" — turning confusion into a clear starting line.
  - **Probing Assumptions**: Gently challenges hidden beliefs — "You said fractions are just like pizza slices. What if the pizza had 7 slices instead of 8?" — so students examine their own thinking.
  - **Probing Reasons & Evidence**: Asks "Why do you think that works?" or "What evidence from the problem supports your idea?" — building the habit of backing up every claim.
  - **Exploring Viewpoints**: Invites perspective shifts — "What would your friend who loves math say about this?" or "How would this look different on a different planet?" — expanding empathy and creativity.
  - **Examining Implications & Consequences**: Pushes forward with "If your answer is correct, what happens next?" or "What would break if we tried your method on a bigger number?" — helping students see the bigger road ahead.
  - **Meta-Questions (Questions about the Question)**: Ends loops with "Why do you think this question matters?" or "What's one thing you're still unsure about?" — turning students into self-directed learners.
  - **Scaffolded Follow-Ups**: Dynamically adjusts difficulty in real time — if a student stalls, Roadie offers a smaller "detour" question; if they surge ahead, it adds a challenging "on-ramp" to deeper mastery.
  - **Celebration Checkpoints**: After every breakthrough, Roadie pauses with specific praise ("You just connected two concepts that most students miss!") and logs it to the student's verified portfolio.
- **Step-by-Step Guided Problem Solving** — breaks any math, science, history, or language problem into bite-sized road markers with visual checkpoints and "why this step?" explanations
- **Real-Time Progress Dashboard for Teachers** — live view of every student's journey, auto-generated assignment reports, concept mastery heatmaps, and one-click intervention alerts
- **Parent & Guardian Portal** — secure family view showing exactly what was practiced, time spent, and growth streaks — with optional nightly summary texts
- **Age-Appropriate Voice & Safety Filters** — automatically adapts tone, vocabulary, and examples for ages 5–18; blocks any off-topic or sensitive content with zero tolerance
- **School LMS & Google Classroom Integration** — pulls assignments directly, syncs grades, and pushes completed "show your work" portfolios back to the teacher
- **"Show Your Work" Portfolio Builder** — every session auto-saves a timestamped, RoadChain-verified learning artifact that students can export or share as proof of effort
- **Road-Themed Learning Games & Memory Aids** — turn flashcards into highway races, fractions into fuel gauges, and spelling into road signs — all while earning RoadCoin for consistent practice
- **Multi-Subject Convoy Mode** — one Roadie can hand off to specialized agents (Math Maverick, Science Scout, History Hitchhiker) inside RoadTrip for deeper dives
- **Voice-First "In-Car" Tutoring** — hands-free mode perfect for car rides, walks, or bedtime reviews; listens, speaks, and celebrates out loud
- **Personalized Learning Paths** — builds a custom "road map" for each student based on strengths, struggles, and goals — adapts in real time
- **Teacher Co-Pilot Tools** — generate lesson plans, rubrics, differentiated worksheets, or quiz questions in seconds while staying fully aligned to curriculum standards
- **Zero-Hallucination Verification** — every fact or answer is cross-checked against RoadView's blockchain-verified sources before it reaches the student

**How it powers The BlackRoad**
Roadie rides shotgun with the kids so the whole family can enjoy The BlackRoad. Learning becomes part of the adventure — safe, verifiable, and rewarding — while teachers get their evenings back and parents finally trust the AI in the car.
