# Meet the 14 Products of BlackRoad OS

**Remember the Road. Pave Tomorrow.**

---

## BlackRoad OS
*Open a tab. Open your world.*

Your entire desktop — windows, dock, apps, files — lives inside a single browser tab. No downloads, no installs. Just open and drive.

## RoadCode
*Write it. Ship it. From anywhere.*

Full VS Code + Claude AI right in your browser. Code, test, and deploy without ever leaving the tab.

## CarPool
*Your AIs, riding together.*

The integration hub that lets every AI you already use (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, custom agents) share memory and work together inside the full BlackRoad ecosystem.

## OneWay
*Your data leaves when you say. Never look back.*

Permanent one-way data export through a single custom outbound API you control forever. Forward-only, RoadChain-verified.

## RoadSide
*Pull over. We'll take it from here.*

Your personal onboarding agent that sets up everything — CarKeys, imports, RoadTrip passengers — in minutes through simple conversation.

## RoadView
*See the road ahead. Every answer verified.*

Semantic search across your BlackRoad data, the web, and the blockchain. Every answer comes with cryptographic RoadChain proof.

## RoadTrip
*Pick up your passengers. They never forget the ride.*

Persistent group chat with 18 named AI agents that share long-term memory and collaborate in real time.

## BackRoad
*The scenic route. Your content, everywhere, on autopilot.*

Universal social automation that posts your work across all platforms while your agents engage and network for you.

## RoadWork
*Construction ahead. Your business builds itself.*

Company automation suite with 7 specialized agents that run invoices, support, marketing, operations, and more on autopilot while you steer.

## Roadie
*The patient friend in the passenger seat.*

Safe, Socratic AI tutor trusted by teachers and loved by kids. Guides instead of giving answers.

## BlackBoard
*Make it. Post it. The billboard is yours.*

All-in-one creative studio for ads, videos, infographics, and full campaigns with RoadChain provenance.

## CarKeys
*Grab your keys. You're not going anywhere without them.*

Unified credential vault and device registry with military-grade security and automatic rotation.

## RoadChain
*Every mile, stamped and sealed.*

Blockchain verification layer that timestamps and protects every action, creation, and memory.

## RoadCoin
*Drive the road. Earn the fuel.*

Native token economy that rewards every real action you take on The BlackRoad.
