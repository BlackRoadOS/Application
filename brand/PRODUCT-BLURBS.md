**BlackRoad OS**
Your full desktop — windows, dock, apps — running in one browser tab. No download, no install. You're already driving.
*Open a tab. Open your world.*

**RoadCode**
VS Code + Claude, live in your browser. Code, test, and ship from a coffee shop or Chromebook.
*Write it. Ship it. From anywhere.*

**CarPool**
AI-to-AI integration hub. Pull in ChatGPT chats, Claude projects, docs, and notes — then watch them talk to each other.
*Your AIs, riding together.*

**OneWay**
Export every piece of your data and route it through one custom API you control forever. Forward only. No looking back.
*Your data leaves when you say. Never look back.*

**RoadSide**
Your personal onboarding agent. Pull over, answer a few questions, and it sets up everything so you can just drive.
*Pull over. We'll take it from here.*

**RoadView**
Search your files, the web, and the blockchain in one place. Every answer comes with verifiable proof. No ads. No spam.
*See the road ahead. Every answer verified.*

**RoadTrip**
AI group chat with perfect memory. Pick up your 18 agents — they remember every conversation, every detail, every ride.
*Pick up your passengers. They never forget the ride.*

**BackRoad**
Universal social + automation. Your content posts everywhere — Instagram, X, LinkedIn, TikTok — on autopilot while your agents network.
*The scenic route. Your content, everywhere, on autopilot.*

**RoadWork**
Company automation that runs itself. Invoices, emails, support, marketing, ops — all built while you steer.
*Construction ahead. Your business builds itself.*

**Roadie**
Safe, patient AI for homework and teachers. Guides instead of giving answers. Trusted by educators, loved by kids.
*The patient friend in the passenger seat.*

**BlackBoard**
Content generation + advertising studio. Turn ideas into ads, videos, infographics, and campaigns in minutes. The billboard is yours.
*Make it. Post it. The billboard is yours.*

**CarKeys**
Credential vault and device registry. One secure keychain for every login, token, and device — always fresh.
*Grab your keys. You're not going anywhere without them.*

**RoadChain**
Blockchain verification layer. Every creation, action, and memory is hashed, stamped, and tamper-proof.
*Every mile, stamped and sealed.*

**RoadCoin**
Token economy that rewards the ride. Create, help, or simply drive — and earn fuel for the road ahead.
*Drive the road. Earn the fuel.*
