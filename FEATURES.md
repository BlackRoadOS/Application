# BlackRoad OS — Product Features

> 14 products. One highway. Every feature earns RoadCoin, stamps RoadChain, and feeds Shared Memory.

---

## BlackRoad OS — *Open a tab. Open your world.*

- **Road Modes**: Switch between "Cruise" (minimalist focus), "Convoy" (full agent visibility), and "Night Drive" (dark theme with subtle highway line accents)
- **App Dock with Live Indicators**: See which agents are active or which products are currently "on the road" with glowing fuel-gauge style indicators
- **QuickSwitch Hotkeys**: Cmd/Ctrl + number to jump between open windows or products instantly
- **Offline-First Sync**: Full desktop works offline with automatic sync when back online; RoadChain stamps queue and apply later

## RoadCode — *Write it. Ship it. From anywhere.*

- **Agent Pair Programming**: Claude (or any imported AI) can join your editor as a live co-pilot with side-by-side diff view and voice comments
- **One-Click "Ship to RoadWork"**: Deployed code automatically triggers business logic (invoicing, announcements, etc.) in RoadWork
- **RoadTest Suite**: AI-generated tests that run in-browser with visual road-sign pass/fail indicators
- **Commit Storytelling**: Every commit gets an optional short narrative summary generated by Cadence for BackRoad posting

## CarPool — *Your AIs, riding together.*

- **Memory Bridge**: Instantly bridge memories between two external AIs (e.g., take a Claude project and inject it into Gemini for a different perspective)
- **Convoy Cost Optimizer**: Suggests the cheapest model combination for a given task while maintaining quality
- **Import Health Dashboard**: Shows which imported conversations are healthy vs. stale, with one-click refresh
- **Silent Observer Mode**: Let agents watch a conversation without interrupting, then summarize when asked

### Shared Memory (core intelligence layer)

Persistent, cross-agent memory vault accessible by all agents — both the native 18 RoadTrip agents and any external AIs imported via CarPool.

- Survives indefinitely (across browser sessions, devices, restarts)
- Not just raw chat logs — structured summaries, extracted entities, relationships, learned user patterns
- Automatic consolidation: agents periodically summarize threads, link related ideas, prune noise
- Semantic retrieval with fuzzy matching and relevance scoring
- Visual Conversation Map / Road Log showing how memories connect across rides and agents
- Every update hashed and anchored via RoadChain for tamper-proof auditability
- Full OneWay export compatibility

### Tiered Memory Architecture (Hot / Warm / Cold)

- **Hot Layer**: Recent conversations, core preferences, active project context. Sub-second retrieval. Deliberately lean.
- **Warm Layer**: Consolidated insights, relationship graphs, medium-term patterns. Auto-populated during consolidation.
- **Cold Layer**: Complete historical archive, raw logs, audit trails. Fully searchable via semantic queries.
- Dynamic promotion/demotion between tiers based on usage frequency
- Retrieval flow: Hot first → Warm for depth → Cold for full history
- Road Log interface with visual tier indicators. One-click promote, demote, protect, hard-delete.
- Every tier movement logged on RoadChain

## OneWay — *Your data leaves when you say. Never look back.*

- **Smart Delta Packing**: Only exports changes since last pack, with visual "trunk weight" indicator
- **Destination Templates**: Pre-built packs for popular destinations (personal archive, accountant, compliance officer)
- **Export Preview**: See exactly what will be sent before it leaves, with redaction suggestions
- **Reverse Audit**: See what has been successfully received on the destination side (when possible)

## RoadSide — *Pull over. We'll take it from here.*

- **Ride Style Quiz**: Short playful quiz during onboarding that recommends optimal agent lineup and settings
- **Template Library**: Save multiple "ride profiles" (Creator Mode, Family Mode, Enterprise Mode) and switch with one tap
- **Progress Celebration**: Animated highway arrival animation when onboarding completes, with RoadCoin bonus
- **Re-Onboarding Shortcut**: "Pull over again" button that lets you redo any part without losing progress

## RoadView — *See the road ahead. Every answer verified.*

- **Answer Confidence Gauge**: Fuel-gauge style meter showing how verified and complete each answer is
- **"Road Ahead" Predictions**: AI suggests related future questions based on your search patterns
- **Memory-Augmented Search**: Automatically includes relevant Shared Memory results without explicit prompting
- **Export as Verified Report**: Turn any search into a beautiful, RoadChain-stamped PDF or Notion page

## RoadTrip — *Pick up your passengers. They never forget the ride.*

- **Convoy Playlist**: Agents can "fight over" or collaboratively build a themed playlist (music, quotes, or learning resources) during long rides
- **Emotion Check-ins**: Agents notice when you seem frustrated or excited and offer supportive interventions
- **Guest Passenger Mode**: Temporarily invite external users or AIs into a RoadTrip with scoped memory access
- **Ride Replay**: Watch an animated replay of an entire past RoadTrip conversation with key moments highlighted

## BackRoad — *The scenic route. Your content, everywhere, on autopilot.*

- **Viral Pulse Detector**: Alerts you when a post is gaining traction and suggests agent actions to amplify it
- **Cross-Platform Echo**: Automatically creates "echo posts" on secondary platforms based on performance of the primary one
- **Agent Reputation Score**: Each agent builds its own follower base and reputation across platforms
- **Seasonal Campaign Templates**: Pre-built road-trip themed campaigns for holidays or business cycles

## RoadWork — *Construction ahead. Your business builds itself.*

- **Crew Huddle**: Weekly automated meeting where all 7 agents summarize progress and propose adjustments
- **Risk Radar**: Legal Lookout and Analytics Atlas jointly flag potential issues before they become problems
- **Growth Accelerator**: Growth Guide suggests new revenue experiments based on your past performance data
- **Steer Mode Voice Commands**: Talk to the crew hands-free ("Slow down support tickets" or "Accelerate marketing")

## Roadie — *The patient friend in the passenger seat.*

- **Learning Road Trip Mode**: Turns a full subject into a multi-week guided journey with checkpoints and celebrations
- **Parent/Teacher Co-Pilot**: Real-time shared view where parents and teachers can watch Roadie sessions and add notes
- **Portfolio Builder**: Automatically assembles verified "Show Your Work" artifacts into shareable learning passports
- **Fun Detours**: Occasional educational games or challenges that earn extra RoadCoin

## BlackBoard — *Make it. Post it. The billboard is yours.*

- **Campaign Road Map**: Visual timeline showing how a campaign will unfold across platforms and time
- **Style Evolution Tracker**: Learns and refines your brand voice over time from your feedback
- **Collaborative Billboards**: Multiple users or agents can work on the same canvas simultaneously with RoadChain version history
- **Royalty Dashboard**: Real-time view of how much RoadCoin you've earned from licensed assets

## CarKeys — *Grab your keys. You're not going anywhere without them.*

- **Key Health Dashboard**: Shows trust scores, rotation status, and security posture of every device
- **Emergency Convoy Lockdown**: One command that temporarily restricts all agents until you confirm safety
- **Family Key Sharing**: Create limited "learner keys" for kids with Roadie monitoring
- **Security Adventure Mode**: Fun challenges that teach better practices and award RoadCoin

## RoadChain — *Every mile, stamped and sealed.*

- **Personal Chain Explorer**: Beautiful visual browser for your entire personal blockchain history
- **Proof Sharing Links**: One-click shareable links that let others verify assets without needing a BlackRoad account
- **Dispute Resolution Toolkit**: Built-in tools to settle disagreements using timestamped evidence
- **Carbon Impact Report**: Shows the environmental benefit of your carbon-negative anchoring

## RoadCoin — *Drive the road. Earn the fuel.*

- **Fuel Gauge Dashboard**: Real-time view of your RoadCoin balance and earning rate across all products
- **Spending Marketplace**: Browse and purchase premium compute, custom agents, boosted reach, or even physical road-themed merch
- **Community Treasury Proposals**: Vote with staked RoadCoin on ecosystem improvements
- **Mileage Leaderboard**: Opt-in public (or private) ranking of top riders by total miles driven
